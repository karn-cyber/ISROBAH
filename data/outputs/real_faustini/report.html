3 · Resolving the CPR ambiguity (the core idea)

A high circular-polarisation ratio (CPR) is ambiguous: rough rock mimics ice. HIMADRI adds the degree of polarisation (DOP), the m-chi decomposition, the L/S-band depth signature and optical roughness, fuses them, and reports a calibrated probability with uncertainty. Ice and rock collide on CPR but separate on DOP.

7 · Method summary

Pipeline: DFSAR(L+S) + OHRC + DEM → calibrate / speckle-filter / co-register → polarimetric features (CPR, DOP, χ, m-χ, L/S) + optical roughness + illumination → physics pseudo-labels → gradient-boosted classifier → Bayesian radar×optical fusion → per-pixel probability + uncertainty → dielectric/abundance inversion (Monte-Carlo) → volume → multi-criteria landing site → power/thermal dash-and-return traverse. Backend: physics-fallback.
